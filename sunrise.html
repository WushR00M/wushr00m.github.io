What is this game?

WELCOME TO SOLARIS

Sunrise is a multi-genre, primarily 2D platformer high-speed hack and slash, featuring the ability to create and share levels you make to the world.


PICK UP THE PACE

On a mysterious day in the land of Solaris, the mayor who has been missing for 4 weeks, returns with the intent of violence, sending shockwaves across the world with a magic device, powerful enough to wipe anything from existence. With this newfound power, he shoots it up at the most important thing, the device powering the artificial sun. Now, with all light gone across the world, it's up to you, Dash, a naive robot and his 3 friends to retrieve the pieces of the device, put them together, and stop the mayor before there's nothing left.


IMAGINATION STATION

The Sunrise Editor allows you to create levels using the same tools we use to make our built-in story levels! Go wild and imagine something cool and new, with new features and possibilities being added almost every update.

THE OVERVIEW

Currently included features in Sunrise are:

  • The Sunrise Editor + Steam Workshop Support* & Playable Custom Levels
  • 1 Playable Character (Dash), and his high-speed move-set
  • The Finish Line, Springpads, Boost Orbs, and more mechanics
  • Chapter 1 in its entirety, including intro and ending cutscenes and 5 packed levels
  • Complete Soundtrack Unique to Everything In-Game
  • The feeling that some things don't feel right...

...but, features coming soon to Sunrise include:

  • Online Play & Level Downloads / Uploads
  • A Finished Story Mode with 5 Total Chapters
  • The Shop, Upgrade Cards, and Booster Packs
  • ...and more planned...

System Requirements - PC

Minimum:
  • Requires a 64-bit processor and operating system
  • OS: Windows 10 / Ubuntu 16*
  • 1.2GHz Processor
  • 4 GB RAM
  • 256MB video memory (Onboard VGA)
  • DirectX Version 11
  • 512 MB available storage space
Recommended:
  • Requires a 64-bit processor and operating system
  • OS: Windows 11 / Ubuntu 22.04*
  • Intel / AMD Quad-Core CPUs (x64)
  • 8 GB RAM
  • 512MB video memory (GLSL Compliant)
  • DirectX Version 11
  • 1 GB available storage space

Steam Workshop Support is exclusive to the Steam version only, and online level sharing and downloading capabilities are coming to non-Steam platforms in Beta.

Ubuntu is the only Linux distro to be measured for support by GameMaker themselves. Support may vary based on Linux distro and distro version.
